Grilled Cheeses Recipe

Ingredients

  • white bread
  • sliced cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Step1: Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet
  2. Step2: Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up)
  3. Step3: Cook untill the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip
  4. Step4: Continue cooking until the cheese is melted
